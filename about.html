About Me

Whether it's hardware like robotics or software development, I find joy in building things from scratch. I have a deep curiosity in the world of technology, from front-end interfaces to complex backend logic, and I enjoy the process of solving problems with clean and efficient solutions.

Apart from engineering, I am also actively involved in a startup project — not just as a technical contributor but as someone who brings vision, energy, and a strong sense of ownership to every task. I believe that passion, persistence, and purpose are the real drivers of innovation.

Every line of code I write and every circuit I build reflects a fire in me — the fire to learn, build, and contribute to something meaningful.
